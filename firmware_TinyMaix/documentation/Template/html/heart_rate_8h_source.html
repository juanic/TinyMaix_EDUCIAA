7  Given a series of IR samples from the MAX30105 we discern when a heart beat is occurring
8  Let's have a brief chat about what this code does. We're going to try to detect

11  data. Build fun stuff with our MAX30105 breakout board but don't use it for actual
12  medical diagnosis.

19  converted to work within the Arduino framework.
